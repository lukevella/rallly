Rallly uses automated content moderation to keep the platform safe for all users. When you create or update a poll, the content is checked against our content policy.

Content that is not related to scheduling or that violates our policies will be flagged and the poll will not be created or updated.

## What happens if my content is flagged?

If your content is flagged, you will see an error message. You can edit your content and try again.

Repeated violations may result in your account being suspended.

## My content was flagged by mistake

If you believe your content was incorrectly flagged, please [contact support](/contact/support) and we'll review your case.
